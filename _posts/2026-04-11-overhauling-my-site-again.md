Hello! This post marks the beginning of the new UI of this site, and I’ve also incorporated the blog into the main page so I don’t have to juggle two repositories and keep them updated. I decided to change my site (for the second time) after I was talking to my friend about our personal sites, and he showed me some nice minimalistic ones. My old site was an in-your-face beige paired with obnoxious blue text, almost all of which was bold. I had also added some animations to my projects, which were laid out in an array of cards, but they were just clunky. I decided, especially after seeing sites like [pbanks.net](https://pbanks.net/), that my site was trying to do too much. Not to mention that I botched my blog by putting it into a different repository which made it twice as hard to update both. Either way, I’ve learned my lesson now. I hope you enjoy the new site!
